About Us

PAN Medical & Industrial Supplies is a professionally managed international sourcing and supply partner, connecting reliable Indian manufacturers with distributors, hospitals, institutions, and industrial buyers across the world.

Our leadership team brings together over three decades of combined industry experience across pharmaceuticals, medical devices, diagnostics, specialty chemicals, and veterinary products. We understand both sides of the market—Indian manufacturing strengths and global regulatory, commercial, and distribution realities—allowing us to bridge the critical gaps in sourcing and supply.

Our Vision

To become a trusted India–Global trade bridge for pharmaceuticals, medical devices, diagnostics, chemicals, and veterinary products—delivering quality, compliance, continuity, and commercial value to our partners.

Our Purpose

To fill the core gaps in cross-border sourcing and supply by ensuring:

  • The right products for the right market.
  • Regulatory-ready, compliant materials.
  • Secure, risk-free supply chains.
  • Access to latest technologies and updated product portfolios.
  • Commercially viable and long-term sustainable partnerships.

We don't just trade products—we build trusted supply corridors between India and the rest of the world.

Why Partner With Us?

Focus on risk-free sourcing & supply continuity.
Support in product selection, pricing strategy, and regulatory readiness.
